FULL STACK PAYSLIP & INVOICE GENERATION PLATFORM PROMPT

Build a production-ready SaaS web application called PayrollPro.

The application must allow employers to create payslips and invoices, generate downloadable PDFs, generate QR codes for verification, and allow recipients to scan the QR code and view the original document online.

The design quality should be similar to Xero, QuickBooks, Deel, Remote, and modern payroll software.

Every payslip and invoice must generate:

verificationToken

Example:

1c7f8e1d-4f22-48c0-a9f9-7f93d63ef111

Public URL:

https://app.payrollpro.com/verify/1c7f8e1d-4f22-48c0-a9f9-7f93d63ef111

Generate QR code pointing to this URL.

Embed QR into PDF.
